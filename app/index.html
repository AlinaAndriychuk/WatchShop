    Zalora

    Zalora watch style is a mixture of Dutch minimalism and modern aesthetics of New York. Zalora was created for free people, and will appeal to both classics lovers and adherents of modern trends.     Zalora is a tribute to the freedom that is in the air of Amsterdam and New York, the cities in which their concept was born, and we are happy to present it to you!
